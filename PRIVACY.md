# Privacy Policy

**Fidcard**
*Last updated: April 6, 2026*

## Overview

Fidcard is a loyalty card management application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Fidcard handles your data.

**In short: Fidcard does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your data stays on your device.**

## Data Collection

Fidcard does **not** collect any personal information. Specifically:

- No account creation or login is required.
- No analytics or tracking tools are used.
- No advertising SDKs are integrated.
- No data is sent to any remote server.
- No cookies or tracking identifiers are used.

## Data Stored on Your Device

Fidcard stores the following data **locally on your device only**, using on-device storage:

- **Loyalty card information**: card name, barcode/QR code value, display color, and format.
- **App preferences**: your chosen theme (light, dark, or system).

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## Camera Usage

Fidcard requests access to your device's camera **solely** to scan barcodes and QR codes from your loyalty cards. The camera is used in real-time and:

- No photos or videos are captured or stored.
- No images are transmitted to any server.
- Camera access can be revoked at any time through your device settings.

## Screen Brightness

Fidcard temporarily adjusts your screen brightness to maximum when displaying a barcode or QR code, to facilitate scanning at checkout. This setting is restored when you leave the card view.

## Third-Party Services

Fidcard does not integrate any third-party services that collect user data. There are no analytics providers, crash reporting tools, or advertising networks.

## Data Sharing

Fidcard does **not** share any data with third parties, as no data is collected or transmitted.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's built-in security features (device lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend keeping your device software up to date.

## Children's Privacy

Fidcard does not collect any personal data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for use by all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
